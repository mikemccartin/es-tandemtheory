## Formality & Register

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Default pronoun | Usted | Professional warmth for B2B executive audience |
| CTA pronoun | Tu (imperative forms) | Dynamic, inviting -- standard for Mexican marketing CTAs |
| Company voice | Nosotros | Inclusive, team-oriented |
| Plural second person | Ustedes | Never vosotros (Mexico) |
| Verb tense | Simple past (preterito indefinido) | Mexican preference: "generamos" not "hemos generado" |
| Headline capitalization | Sentence case only | Spanish convention -- no Title Case |
| Quotation marks | Angular quotes (comillas angulares) | Standard Spanish: used for testimonials |
| Number formatting | US convention (comma thousands, period decimal) | Mexico follows US number formatting |
